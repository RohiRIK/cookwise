Kitchen-OS is your AI-powered assistant for managing your kitchen, from recipe discovery to meal planning and cooking.

## 1. Recipe Management
### Adding Recipes
-   Navigate to **Recipes** in the sidebar.
-   Click **"New Recipe"**.
-   You can manually enter details or use the **Import** feature to paste a URL from a supported recipe site. Kitchen-OS uses AI to extract ingredients and instructions.

### Cooking Mode
-   Open any recipe.
-   Click the **"Cook This"** button in the top right.
-   This opens a **Step-by-Step** view designed for use in the kitchen.
-   **Features**:
    -   **Wake Lock**: Keeps your screen on.
    -   **Voice Control**: Click the speaker icon to hear instructions read aloud.
    -   **Large Buttons**: Easy navigation between steps.

## 2. Pantry & Inventory
-   Navigate to **Pantry**.
-   Add items manually or use the **"Scan Barcode"** button (requires camera access).
-   The app tracks expiration dates and quantities.

## 3. Meal Planning
-   Navigate to **Planner**.
-   Drag recipes from the right sidebar onto the weekly calendar.
-   Organize meals by Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner.

## 4. Shopping List
-   Navigate to **Shopping**.
-   Click **"Generate List"** to automatically create a shopping list based on your Meal Plan minus your current Pantry inventory.
-   **Shopping Mode**:
    -   Click **"Start Shopping"** for a mobile-friendly checklist.
    -   As you check items, they move to the bottom.
    -   Use this mode in the store to keep track of what you've picked up.

## 5. Settings
-   Customize your profile and preferences in the **Settings** menu.
