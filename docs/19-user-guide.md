## Getting Started

### Create Account

1. Go to [cookwise.io](https://cookwise.io)
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email and password
4. Create or join a household

### Household Setup

- **Creating:** Enter household name (e.g., "Smith Family")
- **Joining:** Use invite code from family member

## Adding Recipes

### From Image (OCR)

1. Click "Add Recipe" → "Upload Image"
2. Select recipe photo from cookbook or handwritten card
3. Wait for AI to parse (5-10 seconds)
4. Review and edit extracted information
5. Click "Save Recipe"

**Tips:**
- Use well-lit, clear photos
- Crop to recipe area only
- Works with handwritten recipes!

### From URL

1. Click "Add Recipe" → "Import from URL"
2. Paste recipe blog URL
3. Review imported recipe
4. Save to your collection

**Supported Sites:**
- Most recipe blogs
- AllRecipes, Food Network
- Any site with standard recipe format

### Manual Entry

1. Click "Add Recipe" → "Manual Entry"
2. Fill in recipe details:
   - Title, description
   - Prep time, cook time
   - Ingredients with quantities
   - Step-by-step instructions
3. Save recipe

## Managing Pantry

### Add Items

1. Go to "Pantry" tab
2. Click "Add Item"
3. Search or type ingredient name
4. Enter quantity and unit
5. Select storage location (Pantry/Fridge/Freezer)

### Update Quantities

- Click on any pantry item
- Edit quantity
- Changes save automatically

### Low Stock Alerts

Items turn **yellow** when running low and **red** when out. Add them to your shopping list with one click!

## Meal Planning

### View Calendar

1. Go to "Meal Plan" tab
2. See weekly calendar view
3. Switch weeks using arrows

### Add Meals

**Method 1: Click to Add**
1. Click empty meal slot
2. Search/select recipe
3. Meal is added

**Method 2: Drag & Drop**
1. Open recipe sidebar
2. Drag recipe to meal slot
3. Drop to schedule

### Auto-Fill (Lazy Button)

Don't know what to cook?

1. Click "Surprise Me" button
2. Choose options:
   - ☑️ Use pantry items first
3. CookWise fills empty slots!

### Mark as Cooked

After cooking:
1. Find meal in calendar
2. Click "Mark as Cooked"
3. Ingredients automatically deducted from pantry

## Shopping List

### Generate List

1. Go to "Shopping" tab
2. Click "Generate from Meal Plan"
3. Review aggregated items
4. Add any missing items manually

### Shopping Mode (Mobile)

For the best shopping experience:

1. Open CookWise on phone
2. Click "Start Shopping Mode"
3. Screen stays awake while you shop
4. Check off items as you go
5. Large checkboxes for easy tapping

### Finish Trip

After shopping:
1. Click "Finish Shopping Trip"
2. Checked items added to pantry
3. New list created for next week

## Tips & Tricks

### Recipe Organization

- **Tag recipes:** Add tags like "quick", "vegetarian", "kid-friendly"
- **Rate recipes:** 1-5 stars after cooking
- **Add notes:** Personal modifications for next time

### Meal Planning Efficiency

- **Plan on weekends:** Set aside 10 min weekly
- **Use Auto-Fill:** Let CookWise suggest meals
- **Check pantry first:** Avoid buying duplicates

### Shopping Optimization

- **Group by aisle:** Items sorted by category
- **Share list:** Household members see same list
- **Check off together:** Real-time sync

### Pantry Management

- **Set min quantities:** Get alerts before running out
- **Track expiry dates:** Reduce food waste
- **Update after cooking:** Keep inventory accurate
